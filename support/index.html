How do I play?

Bloomfall is a one-thumb arcade catcher. You guide Remi across the desert, catching falling water, rain and rare golden dew to stay hydrated while dodging brine and brackish water. Reach an oasis and plant a seed to bring the green back. It's gentle and easy to pick up; just keep catching the water.

Is it free? Are there ads?

Yes, Bloomfall is completely free. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no accounts to create. The whole game is just there to enjoy.

How do leaderboards work?

Leaderboards use Apple's Game Center. If you're signed in to Game Center, your scores, measured in miles, points and oases revived, are submitted automatically so you can compare your runs with other players. It's entirely optional; the game plays fine without it. See our Privacy Policy for details on what Game Center does.

How do I replay the intro?

You can watch the opening story again from the in-game Settings menu; look for the option to replay the intro. It will play "The Last Seeds" from the beginning.

What are the shifting zones and power-ups?

As you travel, the world shifts between zones like Crosswind, Inverted, Eclipse, Sandstorm, Flash Flood, Low Gravity and Mirror, each changing how things fall. Power-ups like the Canteen, Umbrella, Sun Surge, Cloudburst and Magnet help you push further.
