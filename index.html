The Last Seed

The world dried out. One seed remains.

You are Remi, a tiny hooded wanderer crossing the Dry — a cracked, sun-bleached world — carrying the planet's last living seed. The only way to keep going is to stay hydrated: catch the falling water and ion drinks, and steer clear of the soda and coffee that leave you parched.

Reach the oases scattered across the sands and plant the seed. Each one you revive turns a patch of the Dry back to green. The journey never truly ends — it just keeps shifting, mile after mile.

Simple to pick up. Endless to play.

One thumb, a thousand miles. Everything you need to keep the green growing.
