Privacy Policy

Bloomfall · Urban Algorithm LLC

Last updated: June 16, 2026  ·  Developer: Urban Algorithm LLC

This policy explains how the iOS game Bloomfall ("the game," "the app") handles your information. The short version: it doesn't collect any. Bloomfall is a small passion project made to be played, not monetized.

The short version

  • Bloomfall is free and contains no ads and no in-app purchases.
  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • There are no analytics SDKs, no third-party trackers, no advertising networks, and no user accounts.
  • Your game progress stays on your device.

Information we collect

None. The developer (Urban Algorithm LLC) does not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. The game does not ask you to create an account, does not request your name, email, location, or contacts, and contains no advertising or analytics software of any kind.

Data stored on your device

Your game progress (such as your high score, the oases you've revived in your grove, your settings, and whether you've seen the intro) is saved locally on your device using the operating system's standard on-device storage. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to the developer or to anyone else. Deleting the app removes this local data.

Game Center

Bloomfall integrates Apple's Game Center for optional leaderboards. If you choose to use Game Center, the game submits your score to the leaderboard so it can be ranked against other players. This feature is provided by Apple and is governed by Apple's Game Center and Apple's privacy policy, not by us. We do not receive or store the personal information associated with your Game Center account. You can read Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Children's privacy

Bloomfall is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from children. Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, there is nothing for us to gather from children either.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above and post the revised version on this page.
